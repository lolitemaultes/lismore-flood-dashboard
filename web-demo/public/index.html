Welcome to the Emergency Dashboard

Would you like to learn how to use this dashboard?

Dashboard Tutorial

1

Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides real-time information during flood events in Lismore.

It fetches LIVE data from the Bureau of Meteorology and other official sources.

Live Traffic Camera
Rainfall Radar
River Height Data
Emergency Contacts
2

Live Webcam & Weather

Access live traffic camera footage, rainfall radar, and power outage information.

Switch between different views using the tab buttons.

The images auto-refresh regularly to show the latest conditions
3

River Height Data

Monitor water levels at various locations with color-coded flood categories:

Minor Flooding
Moderate Flooding
Major Flooding
Use the "Key Monitoring Stations" button to focus on critical locations
Click on any river data row to open an interactive historical graph showing past water levels
4

Flood Impact Map

The new flood map feature shows the impact of current water levels on properties around Lismore.

Click "Use Current Level" to show the impact of the current flood height
Adjust the slider to simulate different flood heights and view potential impacts
5

Emergency Contacts

Important contact information and evacuation centers are displayed in the right panel.

SES Assistance: 132 500
Life Threatening Emergencies: 000
Emergency evacuation center locations are also listed
6

Ready to Go

You're now ready to use the Lismore Flood Emergency Dashboard.

This dashboard is designed for emergency use during flood events. Remember to follow directions from emergency services at all times.

You can access this tutorial again by clicking the "Show Tutorial" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
